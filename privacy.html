Privacy Policy

Welcome to Cowboy Quick Shot! This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you play our game.

Information We Collect

When you play Cowboy Quick Shot, we may collect the following information:

  • Game performance data (scores, reaction times)
  • Device information (browser type, screen resolution)
  • Usage statistics (game sessions, feature usage)

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Improve game performance and user experience
  • Track high scores and leaderboards
  • Fix bugs and technical issues
  • Analyze gameplay patterns to enhance game features

Data Storage

All game data is stored locally in your browser using localStorage. We do not transmit personal information to external servers. Your high scores and game preferences remain on your device.

Cookies

We may use cookies to remember your game preferences and settings. These are essential cookies that enhance your gaming experience. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but this may affect game functionality.

Third-Party Services

Cowboy Quick Shot is hosted on GitHub Pages. Please refer to GitHub's privacy policy for information about their data practices. We do not share your personal information with other third parties.

Children's Privacy

Cowboy Quick Shot is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The game does not require any personal information to play.

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information. However, no method of electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Clear your local game data at any time through browser settings
  • Disable cookies if you prefer
  • Stop playing the game at any time

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our practices, please contact us through our GitHub repository.
